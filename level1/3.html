Never                        seek

       to tell thy love,Love that never told shall be;

                For the gentle wind does move;

                            Silently, invisibly.

                                     I told

                                       my

                                      love,

I told my love,

I told her all my heart,

Trembling, cold, in ghastly fears.

Ah! she did depart!

Soon after she was gone from me,

A traveller came by,

Silently, invisibly:

He took her with a sigh.
